Purple Haze Apartments is state of the art development that will consist of 216 units, 2 similar blocks housing 102 - 2 Bedroom apartments, and 102 - 3 Bedroom apartments each with 12 exclusive penthouses overlooking the Nairobi skyline. The development is located on Kitale Lane, off Dennis Pritt Road, near State House Nairobi.

This is a project by Edifice, a property development company with projects in residential, commercial, hospitality, and mixed use. Purple Haze to be completed in the last quarter of 2017 will be supreme apartments in the heart of the city and outstanding just like our other projects. Purple Haze Apartments, designed and planning by Singapore based firm Wave adding to the international allure of the development.

Leading this amazing project is team of seasoned professionals. The team believes in transforming ideas to reality. They are in charge of the project from the conception stage to completion.
